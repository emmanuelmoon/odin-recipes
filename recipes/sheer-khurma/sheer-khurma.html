Sheer Khurma

The Perfect Pakistani Desert

This festive dessert is traditionally made with dates which are soaked in water overnight. Alternatively, you may use normal dates as well.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup ghee or butter
  • 1/4 cup assorted nuts
  • 1 litre full fat milk
  • 1 cup nylon vermicelli
  • 1/2 cup evaporated milk
  • 2 green cardamoms crushed
  • 1/2 cup sugar

Steps

  1. In a medium sized pot, heat half of the ghee. Add the nuts, and saute for 2 minutes. Remove from pot and set aside.
  2. Add the remaining ghee, and the vermicelli. Roast on medium heat for 2-3 minutes, until golden and fragrant.
  3. Now add the milk with the cardamom pods.
  4. Cook on medium heat for 10-15 minutes while stirring.
  5. Add the sugar and cook another 5- 7 minutes. Finally add the evaporated milk and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  6. Remove from heat and transfer to serving dish.
  7. Sheer Khurma can be eaten warm or cold. It will thicken upon cooling.
